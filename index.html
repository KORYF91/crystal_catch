HOW TO WIN!

HOW TO LOSE!

TO LOOSE THE USER WILL HAVE OVER DRAWN (OVER CLICKED) AND THE NUMBER WILL EXCEED THE RANDOM NUMBER TO MATCH. THE GAME IS THEN OVER
